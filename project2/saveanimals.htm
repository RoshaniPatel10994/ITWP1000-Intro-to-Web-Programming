Save The Animals

I think its important to protect animals and this is the main reason for this website. Going vegetarian is very important as billions of animals are killed every year so we can survive. This is a tremendous burden not only on natural resources but also on entire economies and the planet. Not to mention billions of animals who live their lives in factory farms.

Animals are just like us, they have their own language in which they communicate (which even our experts do not know about!). They also feel emotion (your dog is happy when you come home and animals remembering their owners after decades!) and are sentient beings just like us. They share this Earth with us and they have their homes just like we have ours. They are of course not perfect, but no one is. We need to protect their habitat. You would not like anyone coming into your home would you? Would you not be scared of a more advanced civilization using your body for things you do not want it to be used for? Think of it in that manner.
